A Personal Message From Mike...

I am proud to be part of the Prudential Fox and Roach team. I am proud to be a part of our great community in Philadelphia, a local helping other locals with all of their real estate needs. Philadelphia may be a big city, but each neighborhood is like a small town. From contemporary to classic, hipster to historic, one thing is for sure: Philadelphia real estate has an array of quality properties to offer. If you are searching for the perfect buyer for your home, my tested marketing plan moves homes off the market fast. If you are searching for your own piece of property in Philadelphia, it takes a local to navigate this city and match the perfect home to a buyer.

My reputation speaks for itself and my knowledge of the industry, this city and the current market is sharp. When it comes to Real Estate in Philadelphia, you'll never have a better friend than me!
